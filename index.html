About Us

At Grader, we believe grading should empower
teachers, not exhaust them. Built by educators and
technologists,our platform is designed to simplify
the entire grading process.

What We Offer

• Automated score calculations with custom grading scales
• Easy result entry with bulk upload support (Excel/CSV)
• Student file access for assignment reviews

PROBLEM STATEMENT

Grading students is time-consuming, stressful, and often repetitive for teachers.Teachers spend hours manually checking assignments, calculating scores, and entering grades which takes time away from lesson planning and supporting students.

VALUE PROPOSITION

Our platform makes grading fast, automatic, and stress-free.With smart tools that read student files, calculate grades, and give feedback instantly, teachers can focus more on teaching and less on paperwork.
